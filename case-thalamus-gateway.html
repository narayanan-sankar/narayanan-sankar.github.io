Case Study

Thalamus Platform Gateway + Applied AI

Led platform modernization and built an integration gateway to streamline partner workflows; embedded AI-driven assistance to reduce friction and improve throughput—delivered with enterprise security and operational discipline.

API gatewayIntegrationSecurity ObservabilityApplied AIDelivery discipline

Context

Healthcare workflows require reliable integration across systems with strong auditability and data protections. As adoption grew, integration friction and platform complexity became major constraints on throughput and customer experience.

Problem

Integration friction

Multiple partner systems and inconsistent interfaces increased effort and failure modes.

Reliability + security

Enterprise expectations demanded robust authN/authZ, audit trails, and operational visibility.

Delivery velocity

Teams needed clear ownership, repeatable release discipline, and observability to move fast safely.

AI adoption

AI features had to be embedded thoughtfully with guardrails, evaluation, and cost awareness.

Approach

  • Gateway architecture: standardized external integration patterns, versioned contracts, and consistent auth policies.
  • Security: SSO/integration auth flows, fine-grained authorization, audit logging, and secure data handling.
  • Operational excellence: observability, runbooks, and incident response maturity to protect customers.
  • Applied AI: embedded AI assistance into workflows with clear evaluation, guardrails, and measurable outcomes.
  • Culture: built a high-ownership team and delivery rhythm—0% attrition during my tenure.

Impact

  • Reduced integration friction and improved reliability through standardized gateway patterns and observability.
  • Enabled faster partner onboarding and consistent external interfaces through contract discipline.
  • Delivered AI-driven workflow improvements while maintaining security and operational controls.
